隱私政策

本政策說明 Blackjack Trainer Pro（以下簡稱「本應用程式」）如何處理您的資料。 我們非常重視您的隱私，本應用程式的核心原則是：遊戲資料僅儲存於您的裝置本機，不上傳任何個人資料至我方伺服器。

本應用程式使用 Google AdMob 提供廣告服務，以及 Google Play 應用程式內購買服務（IAP）。這些第三方服務可能依其各自的隱私政策收集特定資料，詳述如下。

我們收集的資料

本應用程式本身不收集、不儲存、不傳輸任何個人識別資料。以下資訊僅儲存於您的裝置本機：

  • 遊戲設定與偏好（如音效開關、下注金額）
  • Pro 版解鎖狀態
  • 廣告觀看次數與冷卻時間（用於執行每日觀看上限）

以上資料不會傳送至任何外部伺服器，亦不與任何第三方分享。

第三方服務

Google AdMob（廣告服務）

本應用程式使用 Google AdMob 提供獎勵廣告。AdMob 可能收集以下資料，用於廣告個人化與成效評估：

  • 廣告識別碼（Advertising ID）
  • 裝置資訊（型號、作業系統版本）
  • 大略位置資訊（依 IP 位址推算）
  • 廣告互動行為

您可以透過裝置設定（設定 → Google → 廣告）重設廣告識別碼或停用廣告個人化。 詳情請參閱 Google 隱私權政策。

Google Play 應用程式內購買（IAP）

購買 Pro 版時，交易由 Google Play 處理。本應用程式不收集、不儲存任何付款資訊。 相關資料的處理請參閱 Google Play 服務條款。

應用程式權限說明

本應用程式申請以下權限：

  • INTERNET：用於載入廣告及 Google Play 購買驗證。
  • com.android.vending.BILLING：用於處理 Google Play 應用程式內購買。
  • WAKE_LOCK：用於遊戲進行中防止螢幕自動關閉。

本應用程式不申請相機、麥克風、位置、聯絡人或外部儲存空間等敏感性權限。

資料安全

本應用程式不設帳號系統，無需任何個人登入資訊。所有遊戲資料均儲存於裝置本機，解除安裝後將一併清除。

兒童隱私

本應用程式含有模擬賭場牌局內容，適用對象為 17 歲以上使用者。本應用程式不針對 13 歲以下兒童，亦不刻意收集兒童的任何資料。若您認為兒童提供了個人資料，請透過下方聯絡方式與我們聯繫。

政策變更

若本隱私政策有任何重大變更，我們將更新本頁面的修訂日期。建議您定期查閱本頁面。

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Blackjack Trainer Pro (the "App") handles your data. We take your privacy seriously. The App's core principle is: game data is stored only on your device — no personal data is uploaded to our servers.

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising and Google Play In-App Purchase (IAP). These third-party services may collect certain data under their own privacy policies, as described below.

Data We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable data. The following information is stored only on your device locally:

  • Game settings and preferences (e.g., sound toggle, bet amount)
  • Pro version unlock status
  • Ad view count and cooldown timer (used to enforce daily view limits)

This data is never transmitted to any external server or shared with any third party.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Advertising)

The App uses Google AdMob to serve rewarded ads. AdMob may collect the following data for ad personalization and performance measurement:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (inferred from IP address)
  • Ad interaction behavior

You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads via your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads). For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Play In-App Purchase (IAP)

Purchases of the Pro version are processed by Google Play. The App does not collect or store any payment information. Please refer to the Google Play Terms of Service for how your data is handled during transactions.

App Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • INTERNET: Used to load ads and verify Google Play purchases.
  • com.android.vending.BILLING: Used to process Google Play in-app purchases.
  • WAKE_LOCK: Used to prevent the screen from sleeping during gameplay.

The App does not request sensitive permissions such as camera, microphone, location, contacts, or external storage.

Data Security

The App has no account system and requires no personal login credentials. All game data is stored locally on your device and will be removed upon uninstallation.

Children's Privacy

The App contains simulated casino card game content and is intended for users aged 17 and above. The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any significant changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the revision date on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
