隱私政策

本政策說明 RoadGuard（以下簡稱「本應用程式」）如何處理您的資料。 我們非常重視您的隱私，本應用程式的設計原則是：所有處理均在您的裝置本機完成，不上傳任何資料至外部伺服器。

收集的資料

本應用程式不收集、不傳輸任何個人資料。具體而言：

  • 不收集帳號資訊或個人識別資料
  • 不追蹤使用行為或位置資訊
  • 不將影片、截圖或任何媒體內容上傳至任何伺服器
  • 不使用第三方分析或廣告服務

應用程式權限說明

本應用程式僅申請以下必要權限：

  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO（Android 13+）/ READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE（Android 12 以下）：用於從裝置讀取您選擇的影片檔案，僅在您主動選擇影片時使用。
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE（Android 9 以下）：用於將截圖及處理後的影片儲存至裝置相簿。

上述權限僅用於應用程式核心功能，不用於任何其他用途。

資料儲存

本應用程式產生的截圖及影片檔案，儲存於您裝置的本機儲存空間（Pictures/RoadGuard/ 及 Movies/RoadGuard/）。 這些檔案完全由您掌控，本應用程式不會以任何方式存取或傳送這些檔案至外部。

試用次數計數（免費版功能限制）儲存於裝置本機，不與任何外部服務同步。

第三方分享

本應用程式不與任何第三方分享您的個人資料或媒體內容。

兒童隱私

本應用程式不針對 13 歲以下兒童，亦不刻意收集兒童的任何資料。

政策變更

若本隱私政策有任何重大變更，我們將更新本頁面的修訂日期。建議您定期查閱本頁面。

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how RoadGuard (the "App") handles your data. We take your privacy seriously. The App is designed with a core principle: all processing happens entirely on your device — no data is ever uploaded to external servers.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account information or personally identifiable data is collected
  • No usage behavior or location data is tracked
  • No videos, screenshots, or media content are uploaded to any server
  • No third-party analytics or advertising services are used

App Permissions

The App requests only the following necessary permissions:

  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO (Android 13+) / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below): Used to read video files you select from your device. Only accessed when you actively choose a video.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 9 and below): Used to save screenshots and processed videos to your device gallery.

These permissions are used solely for the App's core functionality and for no other purpose.

Data Storage

Screenshots and video files generated by the App are saved to your device's local storage (Pictures/RoadGuard/ and Movies/RoadGuard/). These files are entirely under your control. The App does not access or transmit them externally in any way.

Usage counters (for free-tier feature limits) are stored locally on your device and are not synced with any external service.

Third-Party Sharing

The App does not share your personal data or media content with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any significant changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the revision date on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
